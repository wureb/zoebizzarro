Zoë Bizzarro
Zoë Bizzarro is a graphic designer born, raised, and currently based in San Francisco, CA.

Zoë's life has been largely influenced by the culture of SF, visually and philosophically; by the people, architechture and natural beauty around the Bay Area. Her ultimate goal is to promote and advance the unique visual character of the region.

She is experienced in digital and print design and has worked in website design, social media management and branding/marketing.

Zoë graduated from San Francisco State University with a B.S. in Visual Communication Design in 2019.
